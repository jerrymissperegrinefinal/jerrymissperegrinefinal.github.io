Welcome to The Loop: September 3, 1943


While Cairnholm is a bleak, rainy, and unintresting island, the loop is a very intresting place. In place for over 75 years, this is the loop that kept many peculiars safe while it lasted. Notable peculiars include: Jacob Portman, Abraham Portman, Emma Bloom, Miss Peregrine, and Millard. Join us as you witness time repeat itself indefintly as a bomb is dropped on you everyday. Have fun!

  • Recently - Jacob comes
  • Recently - Miss Avocet arrives hurt and with a warning
  • Recently - Mr. Barron sneaks in with hollowgasts and kidnaps Miss Peregrine, leaving the children to fight the hollowgast by themselves and then save her
  • Recently - Jacob and the children get Miss Peregrine back and sail away towards the Mainland because the loop is gone, Miss Peregrine is hurt, and they aren't safe yet
